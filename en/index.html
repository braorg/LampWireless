What's Lamp?

Lamp is a device designed for the deaf and people with hearing impairment who need to be warned when receiving notifications on their devices as messages, calls, etc...
Devised on the basis of the real needs of everyday life.
You can use it on different devices, from the common computer to your smartphone.
Is compatible with major operating systems: Windows , Mac, and Android phones.
Is able to capture signals from any chat or social network you use, for example, oovoo, camefrog, facebook, twitter, skype, whatsapp, viber, wechat etc...

How does it work?

The phone and the computer communicate wirelessly with a device capable of turning on a light upon the occurrence of an event. For phones, for example, the events are rappresenteti from incoming messages (chat programs and social networks), the alarm clock or any other event that the phone is able to report.
For the pc instead, the event support is related to incoming calls voip (skype, camefrog, msn, oovoo, yahoo....).

To start

  • Download and install the application.

  • At startup icon appears on the toolbar.

  • To test the connection with your device click on the entry test of the drop down menu.

  • If the light is blinking, Lamp is connected and ready for use.

  • To stop the flashing light click on the item reset.

Troubleshooting

Make sure that the suspension is turned off in standby mode in Wi-Fi settings "Policy and sleep mode Wi-Fi".
  1. Settings
  2. Wireless and network
  3. Wi-Fi Settings(Advanced)
  4. Criterion sleep mode Wi-Fi
Turn Lamp between accessibility settings.
  1. Access the settings
  2. Accessibility
  3. Enable the voice between accessibility and the accessibility services to enable Lamp
  1. Restart the device
  2. Start Lamp at a distance not exceeding 10 meters from the device
  3. Check the connection
Possible solutions:
  1. Disable the firewall for the application Lamp
  2. Disable the antivirus application Lamp
